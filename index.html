About AquaAI

AquaAI is a smart aquarium monitoring app designed to help you track the health of your fish tank. Using artificial intelligence and sensor integration, it monitors water quality, fish behavior, and species compatibility in real time.

AquaAI provides instant alerts and actionable recommendations, ensuring your aquarium stays balanced, healthy, and thriving with minimal effort.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Fish Detection – Identify and track fish species in your tank using your phone’s camera or connected devices.
  • Water Quality Monitoring – Get live readings of pH, temperature, and other parameters critical for aquatic life.
  • Fish Compatibility Insights – Understand which species can coexist peacefully and receive warnings for risky pairings.
  • Smart Alerts & Recommendations – Instant notifications and step-by-step guidance to maintain ideal tank conditions.

How It Works

  • Monitor: Connect sensors or use the app camera to track tank conditions and fish activity.
  • Analyze: AI models evaluate water health, fish compatibility, and environmental balance.
  • Alert: Get real-time notifications when conditions change or risks arise.
  • Smart Alerts & Recommendations – Instant notifications and step-by-step guidance to maintain ideal tank conditions.
  • Act: Follow personalized recommendations to restore balance and improve fish well-being.

Who Can Use AquaAI?

  • Aquarium Hobbyists: Keep personal tanks healthy and easy to manage.
  • Public Aquariums: Ensure large-scale tanks remain stable with less manual effort.
  • Fish Breeders: Improve breeding success by maintaining optimal water conditions.
  • Researchers: Automate data collection and monitoring for aquatic studies.
